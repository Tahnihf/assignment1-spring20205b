What is Mingle?

“Mingle” provides young adults (aged 18-34) living in or travelling to Sydney with a networking service to help them discover new activities/venues around Sydney and meet new people with common interests. The app uses an Artificial Intelligence Algorithm to bring like-minded people together and suggest activities through analysing common interests and location and budget preferences.

How does it work?

The initial user registration process requires the user to select locations of where they want to mingle and their interests. Mingle allows the user to swipe right (to mingle) or swipe left (not mingle) on each user’s profile that appears. Once the user swipes right, the interests of both individuals are scanned and matches these common interests with 4 suggested activities and their cost for the users to attend together. Both users are then to select which suggestion/s they would be happy to attend and select which dates they are free in the private calendar. Both users can see the suggestions, calendar and their selections. They can then start chatting, knowing what suggestions are of favour to the other person and what dates they are both free...making the planning process a whole lot easier!

User profiles

The algorithm suggests other profiles who have common location preferences, interests and budgets. The user can either swipe right to ‘mingle’ or swipe left to ‘not mingle’ with that user.

App Features

The app includes Activity Suggestions, an Interactive Calendar and a Private Chat on the users' shared portal.

Activity Suggestions

Paragraph: Both users’ interests and budgets are scanned and 4 activities are suggested with their costs. They can then pick which activities they favour.

Interactive Calendar

Users can scroll down the screen to the private shared calendar where both users’ can select their preferred dates they are available.

Private Chats

Users chat privately to get to know each other, discuss the activity suggestions and their availability.
