What is Mingle?


Mingle provides young adults (aged 18-34) living in or travelling to Sydney with a networking service to help them discover new activities/venues around Sydney and meet new people with common interests. The app uses an Artificial Intelligence Algorithm to bring like-minded people together and suggest activities through analysing common interests, location and budget preferences.

How does Mingle work?

The initial user registration process requires the user to select locations of where they want to mingle and their interests. Mingle allows the user to swipe right (to mingle) or swipe left (not mingle) on each user’s profile that appears. Once the user swipes right, the interests of both individuals are scanned and matches these common interests with 4 suggested activities and their cost for the users to attend together. Both users are then to select which suggestion/s they would be happy to attend and select which dates they are free in the private calendar. Both users can see the suggestions, calendar and their selections. They can then start chatting, knowing what suggestions are of favour to the other person and what dates they are both free...making the planning process a whole lot easier!

App Features

The app includes User Profiles, Activity Suggestions, an Interactive Calendar and a Private Chat on the users' shared portal.

User Profiles

The algorithm suggests other profiles who have common location preferences, interests and budgets. The user can either swipe right to ‘mingle’ or swipe left to ‘not mingle’ with that user.

Activity Suggestions

Both users’ interests and budgets are scanned and 4 activities are suggested with their costs. They can then pick which activities they favour.

Interactive Calendar

Users can scroll down the screen to the private shared calendar where both users can select their preferred dates they are available.

Private Chats

Users chat privately to get to know each other and discuss the activity suggestions and their availability.

How to Create an Account?

  1. Select create account
  2. Where are you willing to mingle? (Select location preferences)
  3. What are your interests? (Select interests/hobbies)
  4. What is your budget? (Select price bracket that you are willing to spend at that point in time)
  5. Select a profile picture
  6. Accept Terms and Conditions & Privacy Policy
  7. Complete Digital Verification
  8. Read instructions of how to use the app
  9. Enable notifications
  10. Done!

Other Key Features

  • Digital verification to make sure you are truly who you say you are!
  • Travel mode
  • Get discounts on featured activities
  • Want to bring a friend? Just share the portal with them and they can join in on the app!
  • Connect the main calendar to your google calendar so you don’t miss a plan!
  • Want to suggest an activity? Just scroll down on the activities and select ‘suggest one yourself’!
  • Learn more about the activities by selecting the ‘read more’ icon
  • Have your location/interests/price preferences changed? Just go to settings to change them!
